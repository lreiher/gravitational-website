Great, intuitive design

Owning an iPhone you are holding one of the most beautifully crafted smartphones in your hands. Apps and the same is applicable for mobile games should be designed with an equal effort. Gravitational looks great on your iOS-Device and its flat design goes great with iOS 7+.
Furthermore you are given the opportunity to use intuitive swipes to quickly get gaming. Gravitational is not only fun, but also provides an exceptional user experience apart from the actual game.

Challenging gameplay

Something you should know at the very beginning: Gravitational is harder than it looks. It certainly will take some time to learn how to avoid touching the obstacles by flipping the gravity. But that is not your only objective.
While you are busy dodging the obstacles, you have to concentrate on the simple mathematical task that is being presented to you and do some mental maths!

Show your skills!

At the end of each task you have to show how well did apart from not touching the obstacles. Out of three possible answers you have to pick to correct one in order to open the gate. Commit a mistake and you are game over. Simple as that.
Have you got what it takes to set a new global high score? Get playing now!

Increasing Difficulty

As your score increases the tasks become more difficult. Can you set a new high score amongst your friends? Or even worldwide?

Learning effect

Never had much fun playing those boring brain games? Be able to improve your mental math abilities in a fun and new way!

Free download

Gravitational is a free download including minimal ad banners. For those of you wanting to use the whole screen to play, you can support the developer and purchase an advertisement-free version for a small fee via in-app purchase.
